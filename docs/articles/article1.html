Below we'll show you how the most popular cryptocurrencies in the ecosystem have evolved from January 1 to today, December 7.

Ten cryptocurrencies that made the most profit in 2021

Some indicators give clues about the most profitable cryptocurrencies this year. To develop this classification, some notes from Chappuis Halder and Cie, created by a "team of managers and entrepreneurs in the telecommunications and industrial services sector, with economic interests in various sectors," have been taken as a reference.

  1. Bitcoin

On January 1 of this year, the popular cryptocurrency reached US $ 29,001, and today, its price is around USD$ 51,700. As a percentage, investors who bought Bitcoin at the beginning of the year and did not sell a single cent of Bitcoin got a return of 78%.

  1. Ether

The intelligent contract network cryptocurrency Ethereum traded at $730 on January 1, 2021. Today, its price has reached $4,400. As a percentage, Ether investors have made a gain of about 502%.

  1. Dogecoin

The famous dog cryptocurrency has been all the rage this year. Today it is trading at USD 0.18, and on January 1, its value was USD 0.005685, which means it has increased 3000% in less than a year.

  1. Polkadot

How to know if a cryptocurrency is profitable

Several factors make it profitable, or not, to manage a cryptocurrency in question they are:

The price of the cryptocurrency.

Block generation: the more blocks you generate, the more options you have to earn the block reward.

The number of miners: there are more and more of them, which means that in some cases, it is better to stay away from the classic cryptocurrencies; in the list below are the leading coins and even some more unknown ones.

Hardware: some cryptocurrencies are more accessible to mine than others, and in many cases, it depends on the initial investment, those that can be mined with CPU or GPU logically involve less expense.

Halving: halving the reward for mining a cryptocurrency. In the latest bitcoin halving, the tip dropped from 12.5 bitcoins to 6.25.
